## Building Your AI Employees

<Frame>
  <img src="../assets/workforce_overview.gif" alt="Workforce Overview (Light Mode)" className="block dark:hidden" />
  <img src="../assets/workforce_new_employee_dark.gif" alt="Workforce Overview (Dark Mode)" className="hidden dark:block" />
</Frame>

You can hire pre-built AI employees or build custom ones for your organization. Here's how to create a new AI employee:

1. Navigate to the **Workforce Overview** page and click **"Create New Worker"**.
2. Fill out the **Add New Employee** form:
   - **Avatar**: Select for easy visual identification.
   - **Name**: Choose a name reflecting the role (e.g., "Document Processor", "Research Analyst").
   - **Role**: Define the specific function this AI employee will perform.
   - **Description**: Outline the tasks and responsibilities this employee will handle.
3. Click **"Save Assistant"** to create your new AI employee.
4. The new employee will appear on the **Workforce Overview** page.
5. Click on the employee to configure their capabilities, connect workflows, and manage settings.

<Tip>
  **Best Practices for Effective AI Employees:**
  - Design clear, specific roles that match real workflow needs
  - Connect relevant knowledge bases so they can access your firm's expertise
  - Start with simple tasks and expand capabilities over time
  - Use descriptive names that reflect their function in your organization
</Tip>

### Connecting Applications

<Frame>
  <img src="../assets/workforce_add_application.gif" alt="Workflow Connect Workflows (Light Mode)" className="block dark:hidden" />
  <img src="../assets/workforce_new_apps_dark.gif" alt="Workflow Connect Workflows (Dark Mode)" className="hidden dark:block" />
</Frame>

Set up how your AI employees communicate and what they can access:

1. Navigate to the employee's profile and select **Applications to setup**
2. **Application Type**: Choose email, chat, Slack, or Teams based on how you want to delegate work
3. **Knowledge Bases**: Connect your firm's knowledge so the AI employee can access your expertise and precedents
4. **Workflows**: Assign specific workflows this employee can execute
5. **Templates**: Give them access to your document templates for automated population
6. **Review and Publish**: Check the configuration, then click **"Publish"** to deploy

<Warning>
  Ensure workflows have clear, descriptive titles so your AI employees can accurately match tasks to capabilities. Connect relevant knowledge bases to give them the context they need to execute work to your standards.
</Warning>
