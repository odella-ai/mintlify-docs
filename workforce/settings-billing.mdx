**Billing** compares the worker's saved quote with your current draft settings.

Use this page to understand how model, infrastructure, and operating schedule changes may affect the worker's contracted cost.

## What Billing shows

Billing can show:

- Hourly price
- Estimated monthly contracted cost
- Machine size
- Default model
- Working days and hours
- Scheduled hours per week and month
- Timezone
- After-hours policy
- Service state
- Price override
- Rate version
- Publication key
- Quote refresh date

## Current vs. proposed

When your draft settings affect billing, the Billing view compares the current saved quote with the proposed configuration.

Save the settings to refresh the billing quote snapshot used for future scheduled labour settlement.

## Monthly estimate

Monthly cost is estimated from scheduled hours only.

The estimate excludes:

- After-hours labour
- Separate usage-based charges
- Other charges outside the scheduled baseline

<Note>
Billing is a planning and quote comparison view. It helps you understand the contract baseline before additional usage or after-hours work is added.
</Note>
