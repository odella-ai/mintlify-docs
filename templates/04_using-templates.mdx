## Two Ways to Use Templates

### 1. Delegate to AI Employees

The simplest way to use templates is to delegate document generation tasks directly to your AI employees:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assign Templates to AI Employees">
    In your AI employee's profile, assign relevant templates. Each AI employee can have access to multiple templates for different document types.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Delegate the Work">
    Send a message via email, chat, or Slack: "Generate the client intake form using the attached information" or "Draft the service agreement for Acme Corp from our email thread."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Employee Processes Request">
    Your AI employee extracts data from the source materials, selects the appropriate template, and populates the document using your firm's precedents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Finalize">
    Receive the completed document in seconds. Review the output and make any necessary adjustments before finalization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Workflow Integration

Integrate templates into custom workflows for automated document generation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Populate Template Block">
    In your workflow builder, add a "Populate Template" block where document generation is needed. Select your published template from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Data Sources">
    Link the template block to data from previous workflow steps, uploaded files, form inputs, or connected applications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Output Actions">
    Define what happens with the generated document - save to a location, email to recipients, or pass to the next workflow step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Real-World Examples

### Example 1: Court Filing Form

**You:** "Complete the court filing form using the case details in the attached transcript"

**AI Employee:**
1. Reads the transcript and extracts relevant case information
2. Selects the court filing PDF template
3. Populates all form fields with extracted data
4. Delivers the completed form in seconds

### Example 2: Client Engagement Letter

**You:** "Draft engagement letter for Acme Corp using details from our email thread and the attached proposal"

**AI Employee:**
1. Analyzes email thread and proposal document
2. Extracts client details, scope of work, and fee structure
3. Selects the engagement letter Word template

5. Delivers a professional document using your firm's exact formatting

### Example 3: Batch Document Generation

**You:** "Generate intake forms for all 25 new clients in the attached spreadsheet"

**AI Employee:**
1. Reads data from the spreadsheet
2. Iterates through each client record
3. Generates 25 completed intake forms
4. Delivers all documents organized and ready for review

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear Template Instructions">
    Use descriptive field names and clear instructions in your templates so AI employees understand exactly what information to extract and how to format it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Complete Source Materials">
    When delegating work, include all relevant source materials. The more context you provide, the better the results.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review AI-Generated Documents">
    Strong governance plans should include reviewing AI-generated documents, especially for business-critical functions. Templates ensure consistency, but human review maintains quality.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Iterate on Template Configuration">
    Review generated documents and refine your template field instructions based on the results to continuously improve accuracy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Effective Delegation

<Callout type="tip" emoji="ðŸ’¡">
  **Delegation Best Practices:**
  
  - **Be specific:** "Generate the court filing form" is clearer than "I need a document"
  - **Attach source materials:** Include all relevant files, emails, or data sources
  - **Provide context:** Mention the client name, case type, or any special considerations
  - **Request bundles:** Ask for multiple related documents at once: "Generate the complete onboarding package"
</Callout>

## What Happens If Information Is Missing?

If your AI employee can't find all the required information from source materials, they will:

1. **Ask clarifying questions:** Request the specific missing information with context
2. **Use defaults where appropriate:** Apply default values you've configured in the template
3. **Flag incomplete sections:** Clearly mark any fields that couldn't be populated for your review
