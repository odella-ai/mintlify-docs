## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Firm's Precedents">
    Upload your existing PDF or Word documents. These become the foundation for your AI employees' document generation capabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Advanced Rules">
    PDF templates use AI-powered form field detection. Word templates support sophisticated templating syntax with variables, conditionals, loops, and equations for complex document logic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delegate to AI Employees">
    Simply ask your AI employee to generate documents via email, chat, or Slack. They automatically extract data from source materials, select appropriate templates, and populate documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Finalize">
    Your AI employee delivers completed documents in seconds. Review the output and make any necessary adjustments before finalization.
  </Step>
</Steps>
