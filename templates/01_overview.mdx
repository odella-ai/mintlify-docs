## Understanding Template Types

<Steps>
  <Step title="PDF Templates">
    Best suited for documents with fixed layouts such as government forms, applications, and standardized legal forms. No layout shifting occurs, making them perfect for form-filling scenarios.
  </Step>
  <Step title="MS Word Templates">
    Ideal for contracts, letters, and general documents that require flexible formatting. Features an expressive Mustache-like syntax for defining variables and implementing logic within the template engine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direct AI Usage">
    AI employees can directly utilize templates to generate documents on demand, streamlining routine document creation tasks without human intervention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Workflow Integration">
    Templates integrate seamlessly into your custom workflows and business processes, enabling automated document generation as part of larger operational flows.
  </Step>
</Steps>
