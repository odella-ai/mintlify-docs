# Build your AI workforce with Odella

Odella helps teams hire, onboard, and manage **AI Employees**: workplace-ready workers that follow your processes, connect to your tools, and complete routine tasks with human oversight.

Use these docs to understand the main parts of Odella, set up your first workspace, and start building reliable work systems for your team.

## What Odella is for

Odella is designed for operational work that is important, repeatable, and often spread across documents, tools, messages, and internal knowledge.

## How the product is organized

Odella is structured around the way teams already work.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Workforce">
    Your home for AI Employees. Create workers, review their roles, connect their capabilities, and monitor their work history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Workflows">
    The process layer. Build reusable workflows that read information, transform data, make decisions, draft outputs, and hand work back to your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Templates">
    The document layer. Create reusable document templates so recurring drafts, forms, reports, and letters can be produced consistently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documents">
    The knowledge layer. Store reference material, research, files, and private vaults that workers can use when completing tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrations">
    The connection layer. Link Odella to the business tools your team already uses so work can happen where your information lives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Human-managed by design

Odella is built to help teams increase capacity without removing human judgment. AI Employees are assigned responsibilities, but your team remains in control of what they can access, what workflows they run, and how their outputs are reviewed.

<Note>
  The best Odella implementations start with one clear repeatable process, define the expected outcome, then expand workers' responsibilities as the workflow proves reliable.
</Note>
